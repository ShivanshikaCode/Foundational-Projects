What is artificial intelligence (AI)?

Artificial Intelligence refers to computer systems that can mimic human intelligence. AI is demonstrated when a task, formerly performed by a human and thought of as requiring the ability to learn, reason, and solve problems, can now be done by a machine.Footnote1 There are many types of artificial intelligence that are used today. For example, when a user logs into a movie streaming service, the recommendations for what to watch next are generated by artificial intelligence that uses algorithms to find content similar to what the user has previously watched. Another example is an autonomous vehicle, which is able to perceive its surroundings and make decisions in order to safely reach its destination without human intervention.

Converging technologies along with Big Data and the Internet of Things (IoT) are driving the growth of AI and AI technologies. Machines communicate with one another and are now capable of advanced perception, capturing millions of data points in seconds, processing the information and making decisions.

How to learn about leadership online


While leaders typically set the direction for their teams, they can benefit from management skills to help guide individual employees. Learners can study leadership and management with online courses offered through edX. From tutorials to advanced courses, a variety of educational opportunities are available that can fit different learners’ needs, depending on their desired time commitment and schedule.


Engaging in leadership training can positively impact any job, across all industries. Foundational courses can focus on addressing team challenges, building trust, and navigating change. Advanced learners can take courses that cover specialized topics such as project management, corporate social responsibility, and organizational behavior.
